About Michael Westcott

Michael Westcott is a filmmaker, cinematographer, photographer and editor who has worked in the wedding and commercial/advertorial industries for the last 10 years. He is currently working part time for a video production company – Bullet Media, based in Southampton.

He has directed music videos and promos for multiple artists and companies like Calmac, 29 Studios, Stramash, Payfont, Oban Times, IZZ Juvey, Attack Pattern Delta, Oban Live and Vertical Descents. His film “A year in the life of Stramash” won the best film judges award at Peebles Outdoor Film Festival. He aims to create films with feeling.

Alongside his video work, Michael also photographs wildlife in their natural habitat A few places he has visited recently are Portugal, The Amazon Jungle and Australia. Some of his work can be found on his Instagram page @wildlifewestphotography
