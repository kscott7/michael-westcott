About Michael Westcott

I am a UK based filmmaker who has worked in the wedding and commercial/advertorial industries for the last 10 years. I am also currently working part time for a video production company – Bullet Media, based in Southampton.

I have directed music videos for multiple artists and commissioned for corporate & testimonial films for companies like The Scottish Government, Inspiring Scotland, Calmac, Argyll Holidays, Stramash, Oban Times, Oban Live and Vertical Descents. My film “A year in the life of Stramash” won the best film judges award at Peebles Outdoor Film Festival. I’d like to think that the variety gives me a unique style and approach to each project. I aim to create films with feeling.

Alongside my video work, I also film & photograph wildlife in their natural habitat. I really enjoy being out and connecting with nature. A few exotic places I have explored searching for wildlife are Portugal, The Amazon Jungle and Australia. Some of my work can be found on my wildlife Instagram page @wildlifewestphotography.

I also have keen interests in playing badminton and guitar. Listening to country music is what makes me tick but, I am often listening to Radio one in between to balance out my music tastes.
